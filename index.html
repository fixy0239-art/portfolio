About

I’m a full-stack Lua/U developer with over five years of experience, working on both popular Roblox games and smaller projects. I use Fusion, Rojo, and Knit, as well as Roact, React-Lua, and Roblox-TS. I’ve built tower defense systems, combat mechanics, inventory systems, and more.

I also have 7+ years of experience with Python, Java, JavaScript, Kotlin, CoffeeScript, TypeScript, C++, HTML, C#, Shell Script, and Rust. In Minecraft modding I've used frameworks like Fabric, Forge, and Spigot and worked with bytecode (Mixins, ASM, JIT). I’ve contributed to big Minecraft clients like Feather Client and Delta Client.

Networking libs: Netty, Kryo, Protobuf. Tooling: Groovy & Kotlin DSL, Maven, Webpack. Injection: Guice, Dagger. Reactive patterns with RxJava/Project Reactor, coroutines, and more. Front/back stacks: React, Angular, Express.js, Spring Boot, REST, GraphQL, WebSockets, gRPC.
